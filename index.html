Progress

At its most basic level, progress is the transition from one stage of work to another.

Requirements

Work. Progress requires hard and constant work in order to fully transition from one stage of development to another.

Rewards

Depending on what progress is related to, there may be different rewards for each stage that you complete.

For example: if you were to be weeding your flower garden (or vegetable garden), which is loaded with weeds to the point that you can't even see the flowers/vegetables, your work would likely become noticeable after a very short amount of time. After seeing the progress that you have already accomplished, you would likely become more encouraged and determined to finish the job.

Feelings

Quite often, you will often have feelings concerning the progress which you continue to make on progress. Some of the most common feelings are:

  • Excitement
  • Determination
  • A sense of accomplishment (if that can be concidered a feeling)
  • Successful
  • Happiness
  • Joyfulness

The list could go on; these are only some of the immediate rewards of progress.
